Musician Entertainer
David Hudson

David Hudson is an Aboriginal Australian entertainer. He has a wide variety of instuments he plays and was taught to play the didgeridoo from a very young age. He specialises in traditional music, ambient music, country-folk, rok and new age.

Music was not always his main focus. He attended a teacher's college and becae qualified as a recreation officer. But in 1987, Hudson and a few others established the Tjapukai Dance Theatre and the Tjapukai Aboriginal Culture Park in Kuranda. Also, in 1996 - 2005, Hudson as a dancer and a musician toured with a Greek-American musician.

He was also honoured with a Honorary Doctorate from James Cook University Cairns in 2014. David Hudson joined the Barrier Reef Orchestra in 2016 with their participation in The World Music Concert.
