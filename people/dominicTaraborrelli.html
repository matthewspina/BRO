Accordian
Dominico Taraborrelli

Dominico and his vast skill in musical instruments is one of the most experienced entertainers in the region. Domincio can perform Jazz, Latin, Ballroom, Background, Rock & Pop and much more. Dominico has been surrounded in music since he was 4 years old learning and mastering the Piano Accordion.

In 2009, Dominico had the honor of hosting the Queensland Multicultural Awards Presentation Ceremony at Queensland Parliament house.

Domenico took pride in performing alongside the Barrier Reef Orchestra and The World Music Concert in 2016
