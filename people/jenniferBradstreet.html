Flute
Jennifer Bradstreet

Jennifer Bradstreet has gained much experience in the music industry with travelling around the globe to learn and train. She completed a Bachelor of Music with honors in Sydney and then accepted a scholarship at Saint Andrews in the UK, then in 2010, moving to London. She graduated with a postgraduate diploma of performance in 2011 from the Royal College of Music under British flautist Susan Milan. She was also invited to play in many Orchestra's around the world including Germany,England and Italy.

Jennifer is now studying a masters scholarship under James Kortum and Aldo Baerten in Sydney.
