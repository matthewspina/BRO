Conductor
Raymond Young

Raymond Yong is a Malaysian artist that move to Australia at a young age. He was very young when he learned and trained in music in Perth. In high school h first expressed his interest in conducting when he conducted his school orchestra and choir. Raymond studied conducting at the University of Melbourne and did further training in Sydney years on.

With this training he became the music director the Victorian Youth Symphony Orchestra and Victoria Chorale and was the founding director of Orchestra 21.

Since 2011, Yong has not only conducted many orchestra's fort the Barrier Reef Orchestra, but has also played as a piano soloist, and provided many workshops and lessons on conducting for up comming conductors..
