The Barrier Reef Orchestra: Who are we?

The Barrier Reef Orchestra, an icon of the north, is this year celebrating it's 17th of year of music. BRO is an organisation by the community for the community and over its time has been host to the finest musicians. performing many concerts throughout the year, we strive to increase our popularity, so become a member and check out when our next show is.
