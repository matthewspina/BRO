Goals

The goal of this website is to promote the Slammers volleyball club with the outcome being an increase in people joining the club and sport.

Success Evaluation

The success of this site will be measured through an increase of customers.
The client wishes to see at least a 50% increase in customers before they will consider the site a success

Target Audience

The target audience for this website is 15 to 30 year olds.
This will be shown in the websites design through the use of a more 'mature' colour scheme as opposed to a bright and in your face colour scheme.
Also, this website will cater to the short attention span of a high school student by having all of the important information towards the center of the screen.
